# Butiran Acara — 4 Julai 2026 / Event Details

## Tarikh & Tempat / Date & Venue
- **Tarikh / Date:** 4 Julai 2026 (Sabtu / Saturday)
- **Pendaftaran / Registration:** 7:00 pagi (hadir awal — elak sesak/lewat) / 7:00 a.m. (arrive early)
- **Tempat / Venue:** INTAN Wilayah Selatan (IKWAS), KM 3, Jalan Batu Pahat, Peti Surat 141, 86007 Kluang, Johor
- **Tempoh / Duration:** 1 hari penuh / Full day
- **Hadir mengikut sesi** yang ditetapkan dalam e-mel tawaran. Pertukaran tarikh/pusat **TIDAK DIBENARKAN**. / Attend by your assigned session; date/centre changes are **NOT permitted**.

## Kod Pakaian / Dress Code
| Aktiviti / Activity | Pakaian / Attire |

| **PAC** | Pakaian pejabat (formal) yang kemas — lelaki & wanita / Neat formal office wear — men & women |
| **UKJK** | Pakaian sukan: T-shirt **berkolar**, seluar track, kasut sukan. Wanita: **TIDAK DIBENARKAN** yoga pants / leggings. / Sportswear: **collared** T-shirt, track pants, sports shoes. Women: no yoga pants / leggings. |

## Kos / Costs
Perjalanan, penginapan, makan minum — **tanggungan calon sepenuhnya**. Rancang perjalanan ke Kluang lebih awal (pertimbang menginap malam 3 Julai memandangkan daftar 7 pagi). / Travel, lodging & meals are **fully the candidate's own cost**. Plan your trip to Kluang early (consider staying the night of 3 July given the 7 a.m. registration).

## Amaran Kesihatan / Health Warning
TIDAK DIGALAKKAN hadir jika: mengandung / baru bersalin (<6 bulan) / pembedahan caesarean (<12 bulan); sakit jantung, asma, darah tinggi, kecederaan lutut kronik (ACL/LCL/MCL/PCL), ada besi dalam badan, masalah tulang belakang (slipped disc), atau masalah kesihatan serius lain — kerana melibatkan ujian kecergasan. / NOT advised to attend if pregnant / recently delivered (<6 months) / caesarean (<12 months); or with heart disease, asthma, hypertension, chronic knee injury, metal implants, slipped disc, or other serious conditions — as it involves a fitness test.
