# Persediaan Temu Duga (Peringkat Seterusnya) / Interview Preparation (Next Stage)

> Selepas lulus PAC + UKJK, peringkat akhir biasanya **temu duga** oleh panel SPA. Mula sediakan awal supaya tidak tergesa-gesa nanti. / After passing PAC + UKJK, the final stage is usually a **panel interview** by SPA. Start early so you're not rushed later.

## Format Lazim / Typical Format
- Panel (beberapa pemegang jawatan kanan) / A panel of senior officers
- Soalan: latar diri, motivasi, isu semasa, situasi, pengetahuan perkhidmatan awam / Questions on background, motivation, current affairs, situations, public-service knowledge
- Dwibahasa (BM & Inggeris) / Bilingual (Malay & English)

## Bank Soalan Asas / Core Question Bank (sedia jawapan / prepare answers)
**Mengenai diri & motivasi / About you & motivation**
- Ceritakan tentang diri anda (pitch 60–90 saat). / Tell us about yourself (60–90s pitch).
- Kenapa PTD? Kenapa bukan jawatan/sektor lain? / Why PTD? Why not another role/sector?
- Apa kekuatan & kelemahan anda? (contoh + cara tangani) / Strengths & weaknesses? (example + how you manage them)
- Mengapa anda calon terbaik untuk PTD? / Why are you the best candidate?
- Di mana anda lihat diri 5–10 tahun lagi? / Where do you see yourself in 5–10 years?

**Pengetahuan & nilai / Knowledge & values**
- Apa peranan & tanggungjawab seorang PTD? / What are a PTD's roles & responsibilities?
- Apa itu Malaysia MADANI? Bagaimana PTD menyumbang? / What is Malaysia MADANI? How does a PTD contribute?
- Cabaran terbesar perkhidmatan awam kini? / The biggest challenge facing the civil service today?
- Bagaimana anda tangani konflik kepentingan / arahan tidak beretika? / How would you handle a conflict of interest / an unethical instruction?

**Situasi (kaedah STAR) / Situational (STAR method)**
- Ceritakan masa anda memimpin pasukan / selesaikan konflik / hadapi kegagalan. / Describe a time you led a team / resolved conflict / faced failure.
- **STAR**: Situation → Task → Action → Result

**Isu semasa / Current affairs** — lihat / see [`../01-pac/isu-semasa.md`](../01-pac/isu-semasa.md)

## Teknik / Techniques
- Jawab berstruktur (poin 1, 2, 3), padat, ada contoh. / Answer in structure (points 1, 2, 3), concise, with examples.
- Tunjuk nilai perkhidmatan awam: integriti, kepentingan awam, akauntabiliti. / Show public-service values: integrity, public interest, accountability.
- Yakin tapi merendah diri; bahasa badan positif. / Confident yet humble; positive body language.
- Latih jawab dalam **BM dan Inggeris**. / Practise answering in **both Malay and English**.
- Sediakan 1–2 soalan bijak untuk panel (jika dibenarkan). / Prepare 1–2 thoughtful questions for the panel (if allowed).

## Persediaan Fizikal / Practical Prep
- Pakaian formal kemas, dokumen tersusun (fail), hadir awal. / Neat formal attire, organised documents, arrive early.
- Mock interview dengan rakan/mentor — minta maklum balas. / Mock interview with a friend/mentor — get feedback.
