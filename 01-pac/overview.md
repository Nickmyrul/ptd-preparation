# Profession Assessment Centre (PAC) — Gambaran Keseluruhan / Overview

PAC ialah kaedah pentaksiran berpusat yang menilai **kompetensi** calon melalui beberapa aktiviti/simulasi, bukan sekadar pengetahuan. Penilai memerhati tingkah laku anda secara langsung.
*PAC is a centre-based assessment that measures **competencies** through activities/simulations, not just knowledge. Assessors observe your behaviour directly.*

> 📌 **Format terkini & masa setiap komponen → [`format-terkini.md`](format-terkini.md)** (esei 45 min · pengucapan awam · impromptu · perbincangan kumpulan 8–10 orang). Baca ini dahulu. / **Latest format & timing → read this first.**

## Kompetensi yang Lazim Dinilai / Competencies Assessed (PTD)
- **Kepimpinan & kerja berpasukan** / Leadership & teamwork
- **Pemikiran analitik & penyelesaian masalah** / Analytical thinking & problem-solving
- **Komunikasi** — lisan & bertulis, BM & Inggeris / Communication — spoken & written, both languages
- **Pertimbangan & membuat keputusan** / Judgement & decision-making
- **Kesedaran isu semasa & dasar awam** / Current-affairs & public-policy awareness
- **Nilai & integriti** (etika perkhidmatan awam) / Values & integrity (public-service ethics)
- **Keyakinan & ketenangan di bawah tekanan** / Confidence & composure under pressure

## Prinsip Emas PAC / Golden Principles
1. **Tunjuk, jangan beritahu** — penilai menilai tingkah laku yang diperhati. / **Show, don't tell** — assessors score observed behaviour.
2. **Seimbang** — bersuara cukup, tetapi dengar & hormati ahli lain. / **Balanced** — speak enough, but listen and respect others.
3. **Struktur** — setiap idea ada struktur jelas (isu → analisis → cadangan). / **Structure** — issue → analysis → recommendation.
4. **Relevan Malaysia** — kaitkan dengan dasar negara, perkhidmatan awam, MADANI. / **Malaysia-relevant** — anchor to national policy & MADANI.
5. **Tenang & yakin** — bahasa badan positif, kontak mata, nada terkawal. / **Calm & confident** — open body language, eye contact, controlled tone.
