# Rangka 3 Penilaian Lisan — Buka · Isi · Tutup / 3 Oral Assessments — Open · Body · Close

Buka [tempat kosong] dengan **tajuk anda** — rangka ini muat **mana-mana subjek**.
*Fill the [blanks] with your topic — this frame fits any subject.*

> 3 tugasan: **Instant Insight** (2 min sedia + 3 min) · **Pengucapan Awam** (2 min sedia + 5 min) · **Debat** (10 min sedia).

---

## 🎣 PEMBUKAAN UNIVERSAL / UNIVERSAL OPENINGS (pilih satu / pick one)
Mula dengan sapaan ringkas: *"Terima kasih tuan/puan pengerusi, para penilai, dan hadirin sekalian."* / *"Thank you, chairperson, assessors, and everyone present."*

| Jenis / Type | Ayat (isi tajuk) / Line (insert topic) |
|---|---|
| **Soalan / Question** | "Pernahkah kita terfikir apa akan jadi jika **[tajuk]** terus diabaikan?" · *"Have we ever wondered what happens if [topic] keeps being ignored?"* |
| **Fakta / Fact** | "Hari ini, **[1 fakta tentang tajuk]** — satu realiti yang menuntut perhatian kita." · *"Today, [one fact] — a reality that demands our attention."* |
| **Berani / Bold** | "**[Tajuk]** bukan sekadar isu; ia ujian kepada masa depan negara kita." · *"[Topic] is not just an issue; it is a test of our nation's future."* |
| **Senario / Scenario** | "Bayangkan seorang rakyat biasa yang berdepan **[kesan tajuk]** setiap hari." · *"Imagine an ordinary citizen facing [the effect] every single day."* |

Selepas cangkuk → **peta jalan**: *"Izinkan saya menyentuh tiga perkara — [A], [B], dan [C]."* / *"Allow me to touch on three things — [A], [B], and [C]."*

---

## 🏁 PENUTUP UNIVERSAL / UNIVERSAL CLOSINGS (pilih satu / pick one)
| Jenis / Type | Ayat / Line |
|---|---|
| **Seruan / Call** | "Justeru, marilah kita **[tindakan]** — kerana **[tajuk]** menentukan hala tuju negara." · *"Therefore, let us [act] — because [topic] shapes the nation's direction."* |
| **Rangkap tiga / Triad** | "Kerana **rakyat, negara, dan masa depan** menuntut kita bertindak hari ini." · *"Because our people, our nation, and our future demand action today."* |
| **Gema / Echo** | "Kembali kepada persoalan awal tadi — jawapannya terletak di tangan kita." · *"Back to the question I opened with — the answer lies in our hands."* |
| **Matang / Mature** | "Setiap dasar ada cabaran, namun dengan keseimbangan dan keazaman, kita mampu melangkah ke hadapan." · *"Every policy has challenges, but with balance and resolve, we can move forward."* |

Tamatkan: *"Sekian, terima kasih."* / *"Thank you."*

---

## 1️⃣ INSTANT INSIGHT — 2 min sedia + 3 min bentang (PREP)
**Rangka / Frame:**
1. **Buka + Pendirian (~20s)** — cangkuk + *"Pendirian saya jelas: [pendirian]."*
2. **Poin 1 (~45s)** — Point → Reason → 1 contoh/fakta.
3. **Poin 2 (~45s)** — Point → Reason → contoh.
4. **Poin 3 (~40s)** — Point → Reason → contoh *(atau akui sisi lain untuk kematangan)*.
5. **Tutup (~20s)** — tegas semula pendirian + ayat penutup.

**Dalam 2 minit sedia / In the 2-min prep:** tulis (a) pendirian, (b) 3 kata kunci poin, (c) 1 fakta setiap poin, (d) ayat tutup. / jot stand, 3 keywords, 1 fact each, closing line.

> Buka: *"[cangkuk]. Pendirian saya jelas — [pendirian]. Tiga sebab: [A], [B], [C]."*
> Tutup: *"Maka, [tegas pendirian]. [ayat penutup universal]. Sekian, terima kasih."*

---

## 2️⃣ PENGUCAPAN AWAM — 2 min sedia + 5 min bentang (PEEL ×3)
**Rangka + isyarat masa / Frame + timing:**
- **Buka (0:00–0:30)** — sapaan + cangkuk + pendirian + peta jalan (3).
- **Isi 1 (0:30–1:50)** — **P**oint → **E**xplain → **E**xample (fakta/dasar) → **L**ink.
- **Isi 2 (1:50–3:05)** — PEEL.
- **Isi 3 (3:05–4:10)** — PEEL *(atau cadangan + akui cabaran)*.
- **Tutup (4:10–5:00)** — rumus 3 isi + seruan + ayat penutup.

**Dalam 2 minit sedia:** tulis cangkuk, 3 poin + 1 fakta setiap satu, ayat tutup. / hook, 3 points + 1 fact each, closing line.

> Buka: *"[sapaan]. [cangkuk]. Saya berpendapat [pendirian], melalui tiga perkara: [A], [B], [C]."*
> Tutup: *"Kesimpulannya, [A], [B], [C]. [seruan universal]. Sekian, terima kasih."*

---

## 3️⃣ DEBAT — 10 min sedia (Kerajaan vs Pembangkang)
**Rangka pembahas / Debater frame:**
1. **Buka (~30s)** — sapaan + **definisi** ringkas tajuk + **pendirian pihak** anda.
2. **Hujah 1–3** — setiap satu: pernyataan + sebab + **fakta/dasar** + kesan kepada rakyat.
3. **Sangkalan (rebuttal)** — jangka 2–3 hujah lawan, sedia balasan sopan.
4. **Tutup (~30s)** — rumus + tegas pendirian pihak + seruan.

**Dalam 10 minit sedia / In the 10-min prep:** (a) definisi & skop, (b) 3 hujah + fakta, (c) 3 jangkaan hujah lawan + sangkalan, (d) buka & tutup. / definition, 3 args+facts, 3 predicted counters+rebuttals, open & close.

**Buka mengikut pihak / Opening by side:**
- **Kerajaan / Government (sokong):** *"Pihak Kerajaan dengan tegas menyokong bahawa [usul], kerana [manfaat utama]."* · *"The Government firmly supports that [motion], because [key benefit]."*
- **Pembangkang / Opposition (bantah):** *"Pihak Pembangkang berpendirian bahawa [usul] perlu diteliti semula, kerana [risiko utama]."* · *"The Opposition holds that [motion] must be reconsidered, because [key risk]."*

**Frasa sangkalan diplomatik / Diplomatic rebuttal:**
- *"Saya memahami hujah pihak sebelah, namun datanya menunjukkan [fakta]…"* · *"I understand the other side, but the data shows [fact]…"*
- *"Hujah itu ada meritnya, tetapi ia terlepas pandang [aspek]…"* · *"That has merit, but it overlooks [aspect]…"*

**Tutup / Close:**
- *"Justeru, pihak [Kerajaan/Pembangkang] menegaskan bahawa [pendirian] — demi kepentingan rakyat dan negara. Sekian, terima kasih."* · *"Therefore, the [Government/Opposition] affirms [stand] — for the people and the nation. Thank you."*

## 🧠 Prinsip Merentas Ketiga-tiga / Across All Three
- **Ambil pendirian jelas** (jangan atas pagar) tetapi **matang & neutral** — serang isu, bukan orang. / Clear stand, but mature — attack the issue, not the person.
- **Setiap poin = 1 fakta/dasar** (MADANI, Belanjawan 2026, RMK-13, BUDI95…). Lihat [`isu-semasa.md`](isu-semasa.md). / one fact per point.
- **Rangkap tiga & jeda** untuk kesan. / triads & pauses for impact.
- **Tahu ayat tutup sebelum mula.** / know your closing line before you start.
