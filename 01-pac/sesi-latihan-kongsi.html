Pembukaan Universal / Universal Openings

Pilih satu — isi [tajuk]

Sapaan dulu: "Terima kasih tuan/puan pengerusi, para penilai, dan hadirin." / Greet first.
Soalan / Question"Pernahkah kita terfikir apa akan jadi jika [tajuk] terus diabaikan?""…what happens if [topic] keeps being ignored?"
Fakta / Fact"Hari ini, [1 fakta] — realiti yang menuntut perhatian kita.""Today, [fact] — a reality that demands attention."
Berani / Bold"[Tajuk] bukan sekadar isu; ia ujian masa depan negara.""[Topic] is a test of our nation's future."
Senario / Scenario"Bayangkan seorang rakyat biasa yang berdepan [kesan] setiap hari.""Imagine a citizen facing [the effect] daily."
Sambung peta jalan: "…tiga perkara: [A], [B], [C]." / Then the roadmap of three.
Buka kuat & yakin dalam 10 saat pertama/

Penutup Universal / Universal Closings

Pilih satu — akhiri "Sekian, terima kasih."

Seruan / Call"Justeru, marilah kita [tindakan] — kerana [tajuk] menentukan hala tuju negara.""Let us [act] — [topic] shapes our direction."
Rangkap 3 / Triad"Kerana rakyat, negara, dan masa depan menuntut kita bertindak hari ini.""Our people, nation, and future demand action today."
Gema / Echo"Kembali kepada persoalan awal — jawapannya di tangan kita.""Back to my opening question — the answer is ours."
Matang / Mature"Setiap dasar ada cabaran, namun dengan keseimbangan & keazaman, kita mampu ke hadapan.""Every policy has challenges; with balance & resolve, we move forward."
💡 Tahu ayat penutup anda sebelum mula bercakap. / Know your closing line before you start.
Ayat terakhir paling diingati/

Rangka Setiap Tugasan / Frame per Task

Struktur pantas

Instant Insight (PREP)

Buka+pendirian (~20s) → Poin 1·2·3 (point+sebab+1 fakta, ~45s each) → Tutup (~20s).

Pengucapan Awam (PEEL)

0:00 Buka (cangkuk+pendirian+3) → 0:30/1:50/3:05 Isi (Point-Explain-Example/fakta-Link) → 4:10 Tutup.

Debat

Buka (definisi+pendirian pihak) → 3 hujah (+fakta) → sangkalan (jangka hujah lawan) → tutup.

Tiga sudut serba boleh untuk isi mana-mana tajuk: ekonomi · sosial · pentadbiran. / Universal angles: economic · social · governance.
1 fakta/dasar setiap poin (MADANI, Belanjawan 2026, RMK-13, BUDI95…)/

Debat / Debate — Ayat Ikut Pihak

Kerajaan vs Pembangkang

Kerajaan buka / Govt open"Pihak Kerajaan tegas menyokong [usul], kerana [manfaat].""The Government firmly supports [motion], because [benefit]."
Pembangkang buka / Opp open"Pihak Pembangkang berpendirian [usul] perlu diteliti semula, kerana [risiko].""The Opposition holds [motion] must be reconsidered, because [risk]."
Sangkalan / Rebuttal"Saya memahami hujah itu, namun datanya menunjukkan [fakta]…""I understand that, but the data shows [fact]…"
Tutup / Close"Justeru pihak [K/P] menegaskan [pendirian] — demi rakyat & negara. Sekian, terima kasih."
Sedia 10 min: definisi & skop · 3 hujah+fakta · 3 jangkaan lawan+sangkalan · buka & tutup. Serang isu, bukan orang. / Prep both sides; attack the issue, not the person.
Diplomatik & matang — imej PTD/

Contoh Pengucapan Awam / Worked Speech

"Tenaga Boleh Diperbaharui: Agenda Masa Depan Negara"

Pendirian: tenaga boleh diperbaharui bukan pilihan — ia keperluan untuk kemampanan, ekonomi & keselamatan negara.
Buka 0:00

"Bayangkan sebuah negara yang tenaganya bersih, karbonnya sifar, ekonominya berkembang serentak. Itulah janji tenaga boleh diperbaharui. Saya menyentuh tiga perkara: kemampanan alam, peluang ekonomi, dan keselamatan tenaga."

Isi 1 · Kemampanan 0:30

Menyelamatkan alam & memenuhi komitmen iklim — Malaysia sasar Net Zero 2050 melalui NETR (solar & hidro). Generasi akan datang mewarisi bumi yang kita jaga.

Isi 2 · Ekonomi 1:50

Enjin pertumbuhan & kerja hijau — RMK-13 (RM611b) letak tenaga boleh baharu antara industri nilai tinggi (HGHV); tarik pelaburan (kait TVET).

Isi 3 · Keselamatan + Cabaran 3:05

Kurang pergantungan import & lonjakan harga global (ASEAN Power Grid). Akui cabaran: kos peralihan tinggi → perlu peralihan adil yang lindungi pengguna & pekerja sektor lama.

Tutup 4:10

"Kesimpulannya — kemampanan, ekonomi, keselamatan tenaga. Tenaga boleh diperbaharui bukan sekadar agenda; ia jaminan masa depan negara. Kerana bumi lestari, ekonomi kukuh, dan negara berdaulat menuntut kita bertindak hari ini. Sekian, terima kasih."

Fakta: Net Zero 2050 · NETR · RMK-13 RM611b · ASEAN Power Grid/

Contoh Instant Insight / Worked Impromptu

"How can communication within a community be improved?"

Stand: strong community communication rests on three pillars — accessible platforms, active listening, and inclusive participation.
Open ~20s

"A community is only as strong as the conversations within it. So how do we improve it? Through three things: platforms, listening, and inclusion."

1 · Platform

Create shared spaces — community WhatsApp groups, town-halls, resident associations. Example: neighbourhoods report issues instantly to local leaders via digital groups.

2 · Active Listening

Communication is two-way — leaders must listen, not just broadcast. Example: regular "jom bincang" sessions build trust.

3 · Inclusion

Reach the often-left-out — elderly, youth, different languages. Example: bilingual notices + youth as digital bridges (ties to MADANI's Hormat).

Close ~20s

"So — the right platforms, genuine listening, and true inclusion. When a community talks openly, it builds trust and unity. And a united community is the foundation of a united nation. Thank you."

PREP · pause after the triad · hit the verbs: build, listen, include/

Untuk Team / For the Team

Kuasai struktur, bukan hafalan.

Hafal 1 pembuka + 1 penutup kegemaran. Masa persediaan tinggal untuk isi 3 poin + fakta. Ambil pendirian jelas, kekal matang & diplomatik.
Memorise one opener + one closer; use prep time for 3 points + facts. Clear stand, mature & diplomatic.
