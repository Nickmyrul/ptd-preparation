# Tentang PTD — Apa, Peranan, Tugas / About the PTD — What, Role, Functions

Rujukan pantas untuk **esei, pengucapan, perbincangan & temu duga** — terutama soalan "Apa itu PTD?" dan "Mengapa PTD?".
*Quick reference for the essay, speaking, discussion & interview — especially "What is the PTD?" and "Why PTD?".*

---

## 1. Apa itu PTD / What is the PTD?
**Pegawai Tadbir dan Diplomatik (PTD)** — *Administrative and Diplomatic Service* — ialah **perkhidmatan premier (teras)** dalam Perkhidmatan Awam Malaysia, diurus oleh **JPA** dan dilantik melalui **SPA**. PTD ialah pegawai **generalis** yang merancang, menggubal, melaksana & memantau **dasar awam negara**.
*The PTD is the **premier/core service** of the Malaysian Civil Service, managed by the Public Service Department (JPA) and appointed via the Public Service Commission (SPA). A PTD is a **generalist** officer who plans, formulates, implements & monitors **national public policy**.*

> Sifat **dwiperanan**: **Tadbir** (pentadbiran dalam negara) **+ Diplomatik** (mewakili Malaysia di luar negara). / **Dual role:** **Administrative** (domestic) **+ Diplomatic** (representing Malaysia abroad).

**Gred lantikan kini: Gred M9** (dahulu **M41**) di bawah **SSPA** (berkuat kuasa 1 Dis 2024). Gaji asas ~RM2,250–RM11,110. / **Entry grade is now Gred M9** (formerly M41) under the new SSPA pay system; basic salary ~RM2,250–RM11,110.

---

## 2. Peranan Teras — 4 Tonggak / Core Role — 4 Pillars
PTD membantu kerajaan **menterjemah dasar kepada tindakan** dan menjadi **penghubung antara kerajaan & rakyat**. / The PTD helps government **turn policy into action** and is the **bridge between government and citizens**.

1. **Fikir / Think** — analisis isu sebelum mencadang. / analyse issues before recommending.
2. **Rancang / Plan** — tukar idea kepada strategi (objektif, garis masa). / turn ideas into strategy.
3. **Laksana / Implement** — selaras tindakan merentas jabatan & pihak berkepentingan. / coordinate across departments & stakeholders.
4. **Pantau / Monitor** — nilai keberkesanan, cadang penambahbaikan. / assess effectiveness, recommend improvements.

> Intipati: memastikan dasar **bukan sekadar cantik di atas kertas**, tetapi benar-benar mengubah kehidupan rakyat. / The essence: ensuring policy **isn't just fine on paper** but truly changes lives.

---

## 3. 8 Bidang Tugas Utama / 8 Main Fields of Work
1. **Pengurusan Sumber Manusia & Pentadbiran** / Human resources & administration
2. **Pengurusan Kewangan Kerajaan** / Government financial management (bajet, perbelanjaan / budgeting, spending)
3. **Ekonomi** / Economics (perancangan pembangunan / development planning)
4. **Perumahan, Pembangunan Bandar, Kerajaan Tempatan & Tanah** / Housing, urban development, local govt & land
5. **Pembangunan Sosial & Masyarakat** / Social development & community
6. **Hubungan Antarabangsa & Hal Ehwal Luar** / International relations & foreign affairs *(sisi diplomatik / the diplomatic side)*
7. **Keselamatan & Pertahanan Negara** / National security & defence
8. **Sains, Teknologi & Alam Sekitar** / Science, technology & environment

---

## 4. Di Mana PTD Berkhidmat / Where PTDs Serve
Kementerian · jabatan persekutuan · **pejabat negeri & daerah** (Pegawai Daerah) · agensi pusat (JPA, Kementerian Ekonomi, Perbendaharaan, MAMPU) · bahagian dasar & antarabangsa · **kedutaan/perwakilan Malaysia di luar negara**.
*Ministries · federal departments · **state & district offices** (District Officer) · central agencies (JPA, Economy Ministry, Treasury, MAMPU) · policy & international divisions · **Malaysian embassies/missions abroad**.*

## 5. Sejarah Ringkas / Brief History
| Tahun / Year | Peristiwa / Event |
|---|---|
| 1896–1910s | Perkhidmatan awam SS/FMS di bawah British / SS & FMS civil service under the British |
| ~1921 | **Malayan Civil Service (MCS)** ditubuhkan / established |
| 1929 | **Malay Administrative Service** — pegawai Melayu / Malay officers tier |
| 1966 | MCS → **Malaysian Home and Foreign Service** |
| **1972** | Dinamakan **Perkhidmatan Tadbir dan Diplomatik (PTD)** — kekal hingga kini / renamed PTD — to this day |

## 6. Tangga Kerjaya / Career Ladder
Gred lantikan **M9** (selepas tamat **Diploma Pascasiswazah Pengurusan Awam, DPA di INTAN**) → naik ke pengurusan pertengahan & kanan → **JUSA** (Jawatan Utama Sektor Awam) → **Turus** → apeks: **Ketua Setiausaha Negara (KSN)**.
*Entry **M9** (after the INTAN Postgraduate Diploma in Public Management) → middle & senior management → **JUSA** (Top Public-Sector posts) → **Turus** grades → apex: **Chief Secretary to the Government (KSN)**.*

> Nama gred tradisional: M41→M44→M48→M52→M54→JUSA→Turus; **SSPA (Dis 2024) menomborkan semula gred** (lantikan kini M9). / Traditional grades M41→…→JUSA→Turus; **SSPA renumbered grades** in Dec 2024 (entry now M9). Sahkan struktur terkini dengan JPA. / Verify the latest with JPA.

## 7. Nilai & Etika / Values & Ethos
Integriti · amanah · akauntabiliti · disiplin · kestabilan emosi · kepimpinan · kerja berpasukan · empati · keterbukaan minda · motivasi kendiri.
*Integrity · trustworthiness · accountability · discipline · emotional stability · leadership · teamwork · empathy · open-mindedness · self-motivation.*
- **Neutral & profesional** — berkhidmat untuk kerajaan hari, kepentingan awam dahulu. / **Neutral & professional** — serve the government of the day; public interest first.
- Selaras **Aku Janji, Tonggak Dua Belas, Nilai & Etika Perkhidmatan Awam**, dan tonggak **integriti** dalam **Malaysia MADANI**. / Aligned with the public-service oath, the Twelve Pillars, ethics, and MADANI's integrity pillar.

## 8. Laluan Pelantikan / Appointment Path
SPA (permohonan & saringan) → **peperiksaan / PAC** → **UKJK** → **temu duga** → **DPA di INTAN** → disahkan **PTD M9**.
*SPA (application & screening) → exam / PAC → UKJK fitness test → interview → INTAN diploma → confirmed as PTD M9.*

## 9. Persaraan & Pencen / Retirement & Pension (status setakat Jun 2026)

> ⚠️ **Isu hangat — banyak khabar angin.** Bezakan **fakta rasmi** daripada **teori media sosial**. / **Hot topic with lots of rumours** — separate official facts from social-media theories.

**✅ Yang RASMI / Confirmed (JPA):**
- Skim pencen **BELUM dimansuhkan melalui Parlimen.** Perubahan memerlukan **pindaan akta** (cth. Akta Pencen 1980) yang belum dibuat. / Pension is **NOT yet abolished by Parliament**; it needs legislative amendment, not yet done.
- Keputusan kerajaan **12 Julai 2023**: perkenalkan **kaedah lantikan tetap baharu** demi kemampanan fiskal. / Govt decided (12 Jul 2023) to introduce a new permanent-appointment method for fiscal sustainability.
- Mulai **Feb 2024**: lantikan dibuat secara **kontrak interim**, tempoh **3 tahun** *atau* sehingga kaedah tetap baharu berkuat kuasa; gaji sama dengan tetap; **caruman KWSP** sepanjang tempoh. / From Feb 2024, appointments are on an **interim contract** (3 years or until the new method starts), same pay, with **EPF/KWSP** contributions.
- Selepas itu → ditetapkan ke **jawatan tetap** melalui kaedah baharu (tertakluk prestasi & pengesahan). / Then confirmed to a **permanent post** via the new method.
- **Penjawat sedia ada (skim pencen) TIDAK terjejas.** / Existing pension-scheme officers are **unaffected**.

**⚠️ Yang BELUM disahkan / Not yet confirmed:**
- Sama ada wujud **pilihan "pencen atau teruskan KWSP"** semasa pengesahan tetap — ini **tafsiran yang beredar**, bukan fakta rasmi. Sebahagian laporan sebaliknya menyebut lantikan tetap baharu = **KWSP penuh tanpa opsyen pencen**. / Whether there's a **"pension vs EPF" choice** at confirmation is **an unconfirmed interpretation**; some reports suggest new permanent appointees go **full EPF, no pension option**.
- **Mekanisme akhir masih diperhalusi & belum digubal jadi undang-undang** — jadi tiada siapa boleh nyatakan butirannya sebagai muktamad. / The **final mechanism is still being finalised** and not yet legislated.

**Konteks fiskal / Fiscal context:** kos pencen RM37.4b (2025) → RM42.31b (2026) → ~RM46.36b (2030), ~2–3% KDNK — pemacu reformasi. / Pension bill rising to ~RM46.36b by 2030 (~2–3% of GDP) — the driver of reform.

> 🧭 **Untuk anda / For you:** sahkan dengan **surat tawaran SPA/JPA & pekeliling rasmi**, bukan thread. / Verify with your **official SPA/JPA offer letter & circulars**, not social media.
> 💡 **Untuk temu duga / For interview:** jawapan matang — *"dasar diumumkan, mekanisme masih diperhalusi, perlu pindaan akta; saya rujuk sumber rasmi."* Tunjuk anda bezakan fakta vs khabar angin. / A mature answer distinguishes confirmed policy from rumour.

## 10. Mengapa Ia Penting / Why It Matters (untuk esei & temu duga)
- **Tulang belakang pentadbiran negara** — mengisi jawatan utama (KSU, Pegawai Daerah, Duta). / **Backbone of national administration** — fills top posts.
- **Pemacu dasar** — daripada Belanjawan & RMK-13 ke perkhidmatan di kaunter. / **Policy driver** — from Budget & 13th Plan to counter-level service.
- **Wajah kerajaan** — rakyat menilai kerajaan melalui perkhidmatan PTD. / **Face of government** — citizens judge government by PTD service.

> 💬 **Ayat padat / Soundbite:** "PTD ialah jambatan antara dasar dan rakyat — dan integriti ialah asas jambatan itu." / "The PTD is the bridge between policy and people — and integrity is that bridge's foundation."
